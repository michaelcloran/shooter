Welcome to Shooter

This Game is a basic space invaders remake. Your Goal is to destroy all the enemies. The Game can be played on a Desktop with keyboard keys and on a mobile with touch buttons on a mobile or tablet. BEWARE: The enemy needs 2 shots to be killed!!. have fun!!
On a desktop The keyboard left and right keys are used for movement and the space key for shooting. On a tablet or mobile buttons on the screen are used for movement and shooting.
